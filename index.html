Projects from my practice — with measurable outcomes.

01

Mobile app for an energy provider serving 500K+ users

Challenge: fragmented non-digital experience for 500,000+ active users, high support load, no clear product vision.

Steps
  1. Discovery workshops with business and legal
  2. Mapped all customer journeys
  3. 80+ user stories with acceptance criteria
  4. Supported UX and dev through release
Result: Key actions ~35% faster · Support calls down ~20% · Change requests down ~25%
02

Recovering a stalled IT project that overran budget

Challenge: project over budget and behind schedule. Unclear scope, shifting priorities, fragmented communication between business and dev team.

Steps
  1. Reviewed backlog and current status
  2. Stakeholder interviews
  3. Restructured requirements into prioritized backlog
  4. Phased recovery plan with bottleneck mapping
Result: 80% of tasks closed in 2 months instead of 3-6 · Change requests down 35% · Costs down 70%
03

B2B SaaS for logistics — from idea to first pilots

Challenge: building a startup from scratch. Ukrainian B2B logistics market with conservative enterprise clients, regulated industries, mostly manual processes.

Steps
  1. Discovery and hypothesis validation
  2. MVP scope and product roadmap
  3. Investor outreach and capital raise
  4. Strategic partnerships
Result: $275K capital raised · 9 potential partnerships · 3 MoUs signed · Interest from large companies
04

City digital services platform for government

Challenge: city administration needed to expand online services while navigating public-sector rules, legal constraints, and fragmented legacy processes.

Steps
  1. Workshops with public-sector stakeholders
  2. AS-IS / TO-BE documentation for legacy
  3. Full requirements: epics, user stories, acceptance criteria
  4. Aligned legal, product, and dev teams
Result: Online requests grew to 50-60% after rollout · Processing time down 20-30% · End-to-end automation
