⚡ IT / Project Recovery

Recovering a Stalled IT Project

Challenge: Project over budget and behind schedule. Unclear scope, shifting priorities, fragmented communication between business and dev team.

Action: Reviewed backlog and status, interviewed stakeholders, restructured requirements into a prioritised backlog, mapped bottlenecks, proposed phased recovery plan.

Result: Closed 80% of tasks in 2 months vs. projected 3–6 months. Change requests down 35%, costs cut by 70%.

⚡ Energy / Mobile App

Mobile App for a National Energy Provider

Challenge: Fragmented, non-digital customer experience. High support load, no clear product vision for 500k+ users.

Action: Discovery workshops with business & legal, mapped all customer journeys, documented 80+ user stories with acceptance criteria, supported UX and dev through delivery.

Result: Key actions ~35% faster, support calls down ~20%, change requests down ~25%.

🏛️ Government / Digitalisation

City Digital Services Platform

Challenge: City administration needed to expand online services while navigating public-sector rules, legal constraints, and fragmented legacy processes.

Action: Stakeholder workshops, AS-IS / TO-BE documentation, full requirements (epics, stories, acceptance criteria), aligned legal, product, and dev teams.

Result: Online requests grew to 50–60% after rollout. Processing time down 20–30%. End-to-end automation achieved.

🚛 Logistics / SaaS

Logistics SaaS — from Idea to Pilot

Challenge: Logistics companies running operations manually — no digital tools, chaotic processes, no structured data.

Action: Led discovery and product design, defined roadmap with pilot customers, managed backlog and delivery, built GTM approach for the Ukrainian market.

Result: Launched first version in real operations. Reduced manual work for pilot customers. Validated ICP and product-market fit.
